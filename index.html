Cantrips

Ah, greetings, fellow traveler! As a seasoned wizard dwelling within the Unearth Arcanum, allow me to elucidate the nature of a cantrip. Picture it as a magical tidbit, a wisp of arcane power at our fingertips.

In the arcane lexicon, cantrips are incantations of modest potency, minor spells of the magical craft. They are like the gentle ripples on the surface of a tranquil pond, not the mighty waves that reshape the shore. While grand sorceries demand our careful study and the expenditure of precious arcane energies, cantrips are our trusty companions, ever ready for our beck and call.

With a mere whisper and a flick of the wrist, cantrips allow us to conjure modest wonders. We can illuminate the darkest corners, guiding our path through shadowed realms. We ignite sparks, summoning flames that dance upon our fingertips, granting warmth and light at a moment's notice. We can evoke breezes, a gentle gust to sway a curtain or ruffle the hair of an unsuspecting companion.

Though their power is humble, cantrips are invaluable tools. In times of strife, we can conjure spectral hands to manipulate objects or mend minor wounds. We craft illusions, playful tricks to amuse or confuse. A whispered word, and we can send forth crackling energy to strike our adversaries, reminding them of our magical might.

Unlike our more arduous spells, cantrips are ever present, an extension of our arcane essence. They require no intricate preparation or deplete our reservoirs of magic. We can cast them effortlessly, weaving magic into the tapestry of our daily lives. Their potency may be limited, but their versatility is a treasure we wizards cherish.

So, my friend, as you wander through the realms, remember the humble cantrip. It is the constant companion of wizards, a manifestation of our magical prowess in its simplest and most accessible form.

First Level Spells

Ah, first level spells, the gateway to the realm of true arcane wonders! As a wizard, these spells mark the beginning of our journey into the realms of significant magical prowess.

Unlike cantrips, first level spells possess a tangible potency that transcends the realm of minor tricks. They embody the fruits of our diligent studies and tireless pursuit of arcane knowledge. With each new spell mastered, we unlock a door to a world of possibilities, granting us the ability to shape reality with our enchanted words and gestures.

First level spells are more substantial in their effects, offering a range of options to suit our needs. They grant us the power to hurl bolts of magical energy, unleash elemental forces, obscure ourselves from prying eyes, heal wounds with mystical energies, or charm the minds of others. These spells can alter the very fabric of reality within their limited scope.

However, it is important to remember that the power of first level spells comes with a price. Unlike cantrips, which we can cast at will, these spells require careful preparation and expenditure of our limited magical resources. Before embarking on our adventures, we must meticulously select the spells we deem most useful, preparing them in our spellbooks or committing them to memory.

Additionally, the casting of first level spells requires us to channel our arcane energies through the use of spell slots. These slots represent our reservoir of magical power, and once expended, they must be replenished through rest or other means. Thus, we must choose our spells judiciously, considering the challenges that lie ahead and the resources at our disposal.

First level spells, dear companion, are the building blocks of our magical repertoire. They grant us a glimpse into the boundless potential of arcane might. As we continue our journeys and delve deeper into the secrets of the arcane arts, these spells will be but the beginning, paving the way for even greater sorceries that await us in the hallowed realms of higher levels.

Spell Scroll Guide

Welcome to the Spell Scroll Shop!

Here, the arcane arts come to life! Discover a wide selection of spell scrolls imbued with potent magic, waiting to be unleashed by aspiring spellcasters like yourself. Let us guide you through the process of acquiring and effectively utilizing these powerful tools.

1. Acquiring Spell Scrolls:

To purchase a spell scroll, simply reach out to Arlun or another member of the Arcane Accord. Contact us and express your desire to acquire a scroll of your choice. We will then make the necessary arrangements to have your selected scroll delivered to you promptly. Keep in mind that special discounts are available if you bring valuable resources like Sandstone or specific ingredients listed in our pricing guide.

2. Available Spells:

At our shop, we stock a range of spells from Cantrips to Level 2 spells, designed specifically for wizards. Whether you seek minor magical manipulations or more significant arcane powers, you'll find scrolls suitable for your needs. Explore our shelves and discover the mystic wonders that await you.

3. Utilizing Spell Scrolls:

To activate a spell scroll, follow these steps:

  1. Break the Glass Vial: Each spell scroll is carefully enclosed within a glass vial. Gently break the vial to access the scroll held within. Exercise caution to prevent any damage to the scroll itself.
  2. INT Check: Engage your intellect by attempting a simple Intelligence (INT) check equal to 4 + the level of the spell. This test ensures that you can comprehend and properly harness the magical essence contained within the scroll. Success allows you to proceed to the next step. However failure means the spell is lost and a Wild Magic surge.
  3. Follow Instructions: Unfurled before you is the intricate script detailing the spell's instructions. Read and comprehend the instructions meticulously, paying attention to the subtle nuances that govern the successful casting of the spell.
  4. Casting Time: The casting time for a spell scroll mirrors that of the original spell it represents. Whether it requires a single action, bonus action, reaction, or even a minute of focused concentration, adhere to the specified timing to unleash the scroll's magical effects.

Remember, utilizing a spell scroll grants you access to powerful magic normally beyond your grasp. However, it is crucial to approach this arcane endeavor with caution and respect. Carefully preserve and protect your spell scrolls to ensure their continued efficacy. Handle them with care, shielding them from harm to preserve their magical integrity.

At The Sponsor of Scholars, we pride ourselves on providing aspiring spellcasters like you with the means to embark on extraordinary magical journeys. May your encounters with our spell scrolls bring enlightenment, wonder, and triumph as you delve into the realms of potent sorcery.
